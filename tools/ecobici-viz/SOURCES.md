## Sources

- **Trip data** — [Ecobici open data](https://ecobici.cdmx.gob.mx/en/open-data/), Mexico City's bike-share system. Monthly CSVs of anonymized trips (station, date, time).
- **Station coordinates** — [GBFS](https://gbfs.mex.lyftbikes.com/gbfs/gbfs.json) (`station_information.json`), the standard bike-share data feed.
- **Rendering** — adapted from this site's own `public/background.js`.
- **Encoding** — [ffmpeg](https://ffmpeg.org/) (MP4) and [libwebp](https://developers.google.com/speed/webp)'s `img2webp` (WebP).
